$$
\begin{align}
\frac{d}{db}\left[b\log_bx\right] &= \frac{d}{db}\left[b\frac{\ln x}{\ln b}\right] = \ln x\frac{d}{db}\left[\frac{b}{\ln b}\right] \\
                                  &= \ln x\frac{\frac{d}{db}\left[b\right]\cdot\ln b-b\cdot\frac{d}{db}\left[\ln b\right]}{\ln^2b} \\
                                  &= \ln x\frac{\ln b-1}{\ln^2b} = \ln x\left(\frac{1}{\ln b}-\frac{1}{\ln^2b}\right),
\end{align}
$$

which has a zero at $b=e$, so the function $b\log_bx$ achieves its minimum value at $b=e$, because if $1<b<e$, then $\frac{1}{\ln b}<\frac{1}{\ln^2b}$ and if $e<b$, then $\frac{1}{\ln b}>\frac{1}{\ln^2b}$.

We need to compare $2\log_2x$ and $3\log_3x$, as $2<e<3$.

$$
2\log_2x=2\frac{\ln x}{\ln 2} = \frac{2}{\ln2}\ln x > \frac{3}{\ln 3}\ln x = 3\frac{\ln x}{\ln 3}=3\ln_3x,
$$

because

$$
\frac{2}{\ln 2}>\frac{3}{\ln 3},
$$

because

$$
2\ln3 = \ln3^2 = \ln9 > \ln8 = \ln2^3 = 3\ln2.
$$
