> What are $\lfloor1.1\rfloor$, $\lfloor-1.1\rfloor$, $\lceil-1.1\rceil$, $\lfloor0.99999\rfloor$, and $\lfloor\lg35\rfloor$?

---

Clearly $\lfloor1.1\rfloor=1$, $\lfloor-1.1\rfloor=-2$, $\lceil-1.1\rceil=-1$, and $\lfloor0.99999\rfloor=0$.

It follows from $\lg32=5$ and $\lg64=6$ that $5<\lg35<6$, and therefore $\lfloor\lg35\rfloor=5$.
